About Me

Hi, I’m Nafisa! I’m currently in my second year, double majoring in Communication, Culture, Information and Technology (CCIT) and Technology, Coding and Society (TCS) at the University of Toronto. I am deeply passionate about Technology and Creative Arts. I also love taking photos whenever I go outside!

When it comes to my professional life, I enjoy tackling complex challenges, designing user-centered solutions, and creating experiences that blend functionality with aesthetic appeal. After graduation, I aspire to start a career in User Experience (UX) Design, where I can contribute to crafting intuitive and meaningful interactions that enhance the way people engage with technology.
